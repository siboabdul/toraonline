1. What is the online voting system?

The online voting system is a digital platform that allows eligible voters to cast their votes securely and conveniently through the internet.

2. Is online voting secure?

Yes, we prioritize the security of the online voting system. We employ robust encryption protocols, authentication mechanisms, and other security measures to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of the voting process.

3. How can I participate in online voting?

To participate in online voting, you need to be a registered voter. You will receive login credentials or a unique voting link via email or other designated communication channels. Simply follow the instructions provided to access the online voting platform and cast your vote.

4. What are the advantages of online voting?

Online voting offers several advantages, including convenience, accessibility for remote voters, reduced costs, and faster results tabulation. It also minimizes paper usage and streamlines the overall voting process.

5. Can I change my vote after submitting it online?

No, once you submit your vote online, it is considered final and cannot be changed. Make sure to review your choices before submitting.

We provide a confirmation message or receipt after you successfully cast your vote online. Additionally, we have stringent auditing and verification processes in place to ensure the accuracy and transparency of the voting results.
