Requirements for Presidential candidates
A candidate for the office of the President of the Republic should:

  • be of a Rwandan nationality by origin;

  • not hold any other nationality;

  • be of irreproachable in his or her conduct and social relations;

  • not to have been definitely sentenced to a term of imprisonment equal to or exceeding six (6) months;

  • not to have been deprived of his or her civil and political rights;

  • be at least thirty-five (35) years of age on the date of the submission of his or her candidature;

  • be resident in Rwanda at the time of submitting his or her candidacy.

Elements to be included in a Presidential candidate dossier

The candidature for the office of the President of the Republic shall be comprised of the following:

  1. the candidate names as indicated in his/her identity card;

  2. document indicating that the candidate is a flag bearer of a Political Organisation or a Coalition of
    Political Organisations established in accordance with the law, or that he or she is an independent
    candidate or a candidate supported by several Political Organisations;

  3. his/ her curriculum vitae indicating his or her profession, residence and positions previously held;

  4. two (2) coloured passport sized photographs;

  5. a copy of his or her national identity card;

  6. a copy of his or her voter’s card;

  7. a logo to identify him or her on a ballot paper.

    8. Documents required from the candidate are submitted, in person, in two (2) copies, one reserved for the National Electoral Commission and another one sealed with the seal of the National Electoral Commission on each page and given back to the candidate.
    An independent candidate shall submit a list of at least six hundred (600) eligible voters endorsing his or her candidature, with at least twelve (12) persons registered in each District.

special requirement

Every person endorsing his or her candidature shall sign or fingerprint before his or her name, and the list shall indicate the following:

  1. full names of the person who signed or fingerprinted the list.
  2. the number of his or her national identity card and place of issue.
  3. the number of his or her voter’s card and where it was issued.
  4. place of residence (District, Sector, Cell and Village); and any other information that may be required by the National Electoral Commission.
  5. Persons prohibited from registering on the Voters’ register should not sign or fingerprint on the list of persons supporting the candidate.
  6. An independent candidate should be an eligible voter, registered on the voters’ register and possess a voter’s card.
  7. The submission of the candidature for the office of the President of the Republic is deposited personally, in writing, and it is submitted to the National Electoral Commission which issues an acknowledgement of receipt thereof.
